GAME BALANCE & PROGRESSION
================================================================================

Player Stats (Base):
  - HP: 100 (scales +10 per level)
  - Damage: 10 (modified by buffs)
  - Speed: 2 (modified by buffs)
  - Defense: 1.0 (modified by buffs)
  - Attack Cooldown: 0.5 seconds

Enemy Progression (per level):
  - Level 1: 4 enemies, HP/DMG base values
  - Level 2: 5 enemies, ~1.2x stat multiplier
  - Level 3: 6 enemies, ~1.4x stat multiplier
  - Level N: (3+N) enemies, 1+(N-1)*0.2 multiplier

Level-Up System:
  - Triggers: Every 10 kills per level
  - Player Reward: +10 max HP, full heal
  - World Reaction: Dungeon regeneration
  - Enemy Reaction: New spawn with scaled stats
  - Score Impact: Points per kill multiplied by level factor

Ability Balancing:
  - Dash: 3s cooldown, defensive utility
  - Cleave: 5s cooldown, offensive AoE
  - Heal: 15s cooldown, support action
  - No ability cooldown overlaps prevent spam

HOW TO PLAY
================================================================================

1. Open DarkFantasyGame.html in any modern web browser
2. Use arrow keys or WASD to move
3. Walk into enemies to attack (auto-attack at 0.5s intervals)
4. Collect colored substances to gain buffs
5. Press SPACE to dash, E for cleave AoE, H to heal
6. Defeat 10 enemies to level up and progress
7. Try to reach the highest level possible
8. On death, enter your name to join the leaderboard

Win Condition: Reach the highest possible level
Lose Condition: Health drops to 0
